Meet the Founder

Adedayo Mariam Omowumi

Founder & Creative Director

At Elegant Fashion Palace, fashion is more than clothing — it is a statement of confidence, beauty, and individuality. Founded by a passionate fashion designer with a deep love for style and craftsmanship, this brand was created with one mission: to make every woman feel elegant, confident, and luxurious in what she wears.

What started as a dream and a passion for designing beautiful outfits has grown into a fashion brand dedicated to creating stylish ready-to-wear pieces and stunning jewellery accessories. Every design is thoughtfully crafted to combine comfort, elegance, and modern trends — ensuring our customers always stand out.

From flowing Bubu gowns, stylish jumbo pants, and elegant maxi skirts to beautiful kimonos and carefully selected jewellery accessories, each piece reflects sophistication and timeless beauty. We believe fashion should make you feel special — and we pay attention to every detail, from fabric selection to finishing touches.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🧵

Craftsmanship First

Every stitch is intentional. We do not rush our work. Every hem is hand-finished, every seam pressed, every detail considered. Our reputation is built one garment at a time.

🌍

African Pride

We celebrate African textiles — Ankara, adire, aso-oke, kente — not as trends, but as heritage. We source locally, employ locally, and tell African stories through every design.

💛

Inclusivity Always

We believe every body is a canvas for great fashion. We do not have a standard size — we have your size. From petite to plus, young to elderly, every client is treated with the same attention and care.

🤝

Trust & Transparency

We tell you what's possible and what isn't. We give realistic timelines. We answer every WhatsApp message. We believe great fashion relationships are built on honest communication.

👶

Family at the Centre

From children's birthday outfits to bridal gowns, from couples' pre-wedding looks to grandmother's Iro & Buba — we dress the whole family, and we take that responsibility seriously.

♻️

Thoughtful Production

We make only what is ordered. We minimise waste by using every fabric scrap in smaller accessories. We prefer natural fibres where possible and support local artisans who share our values.
