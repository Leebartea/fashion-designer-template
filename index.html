The Process

How Bespoke Works

From first consultation to final fitting — a seamless, personal experience crafted around you.

1

Consultation

Chat with us on WhatsApp or visit the studio. Share your vision, occasion, and inspiration photos.

2

Measurements

Visit us for in-studio measurements or use our guided self-measurement form. Every inch matters.

3

Craft & Fitting

Our artisans cut, sew, and hand-finish your piece. 1–2 fitting sessions ensure a perfect result.

4

Delivery

Collect from the studio or we ship to your door — Lagos and nationwide. International shipping available.

The Studio

Where Fabric Becomes Story

In our Surulere studio, every piece begins as a conversation. We listen to your story — your occasion, your body, your culture — and translate it into fabric, cut, and craft.

From sourcing premium Dutch Wax Ankara at Balogun Market to hand-sewing the final hem, every step is done with intention. We believe fashion should feel personal, not mass-produced.
