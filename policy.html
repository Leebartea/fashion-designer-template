Returns & Exchanges

Ready-to-Wear Items: We accept returns or exchanges within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unworn, unwashed, and has all original tags attached. The customer is responsible for return shipping costs. Items returned outside this window or without prior notice will not be accepted.

Bespoke & Custom Orders: All bespoke orders are non-refundable as they are made specifically for you. However, if there is a clear error on our part (wrong size, wrong fabric, visible defect), we will remake the garment at no extra cost. Please report any issues within 48 hours of receiving your order, with photographs.

Sale Items: All items purchased during a sale or at a discounted rate are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to Initiate a Return: Send a WhatsApp message to our studio with your order details, photos of the item, and the reason for return. We will respond within 24 hours (Mon–Sat) with instructions.

Alterations

We offer alteration services for items purchased from us. Minor alterations (hem, waist, sleeve length) are offered at a discounted rate. Significant alterations (restructuring, re-fitting) are priced on request.

Alteration work is done at our studio in Ibadan, Oyo. For out-of-state clients, please contact us for a remote assessment. We will advise whether the alteration can be done without an in-studio visit.

Turnaround for Alterations: 3–10 business days depending on complexity and current workload. Rush alterations may be available with an additional fee.

Fabric Care Instructions

Ankara (Cotton Wax Print)

  • • Hand wash or machine wash on gentle/delicate cycle in cold or lukewarm water (max 30°C)
  • • Use mild liquid detergent — avoid powder detergents and bleach
  • • Do not soak for extended periods
  • • Do not tumble dry — air dry in shade to preserve colour intensity
  • • Iron on medium heat (cotton setting) while slightly damp, or use a pressing cloth
  • • Store folded or hung — avoid crushing for long periods

Adire & Batik (Hand-Dyed)

  • • Hand wash only in cold water — never machine wash
  • • Wash separately for first 2–3 washes as natural dyes may transfer
  • • Use a mild soap or specialist fabric dye wash
  • • Do not wring — gently squeeze and roll in a clean towel
  • • Air dry flat in shade
  • • Iron on low-medium heat using a pressing cloth

Lace, Chiffon & Formal Fabrics

  • • Dry cleaning is strongly recommended for lace and beaded formal garments
  • • If hand washing is necessary: cold water only, very gentle soap, do not rub
  • • Never wring lace — gently press between clean towels to remove moisture
  • • Lay flat to dry on a clean white towel — never hang lace wet
  • • Iron on wrong side using a pressing cloth — never iron beaded areas
  • • Store in a breathable garment bag — never in plastic

Velvet

  • • Dry clean only for most velvet garments
  • • If spot cleaning: dab gently — never rub as this will crush the pile
  • • Steam gently to revive the pile — never iron directly
  • • Store hung or laid flat — never folded, as fold lines in velvet are very difficult to remove

Payment Policy

Accepted Payment Methods: Bank transfer (Zenith, GT Bank, Access Bank — details shared via WhatsApp), cash in-studio, Opay, Palmpay. International: Wise, Payoneer, or bank wire transfer.

Currency: All prices are listed in Nigerian Naira (₦). International clients are quoted in their local currency using the prevailing exchange rate at the time of order.
