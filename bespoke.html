The Process

How Bespoke Works

Five steps from first conversation to wearing your perfect piece.

1

Consultation

Chat via WhatsApp or visit our studio. Share your vision, inspiration photos, and occasion.

2

Measurements

Visit us in Surulere or use our guided measurement form below. We accept inches or centimetres.

3

Fabric Choice

Choose from our curated fabric library — Ankara, adire, lace, satin, chiffon — or supply your own.

4

Fitting

1–2 fitting sessions ensure every seam, curve, and hemline is exactly right before final completion.

5

Collection

Collect from our studio or we ship to your door. Nationwide and international shipping available.

Dutch Wax Ankara

Premium Dutch Wax Ankara in hundreds of prints. Bold, vibrant, and iconic West African fabric. Sourced from Balogun Market and verified Dutch importers.

  • ✓ Excellent colour fastness
  • ✓ Dozens of new prints each season
  • ✓ 100% cotton — cool and breathable

Adire & Batik

Traditional hand-dyed Nigerian adire and Indonesian batik fabrics. Each piece is unique — no two are exactly the same. Deep, rich indigo, terracotta, and earthy tones.

  • ✓ Natural dyes, sustainable production
